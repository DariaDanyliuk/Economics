Why am I studying economics?

I study economics in order to then understand how to make money, how to build my own business. In economics, we are taught what money is, what economics studies and how to interact with her.

In the lessons, we think what kind of mini business we want to create. We get something, and we translate it into real life. But something remains unfinished and impossible. Some even managed to earn money, although not much. But in most cases, nothing worked. It even happened that a thought would come to mind that could not be translated into reality, and it is unlikely that Elon Musk himself was able to create such a thing.
